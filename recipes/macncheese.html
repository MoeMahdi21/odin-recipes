Mac & Cheese

Description

This creamy mac and cheese is a comfort food classic. Made with tender pasta and rich, cheesy sauce, it's perfect for any day of the week.

Ready in about 30 minutes, itâ€™s simple, delicious, and loved by all ages.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni according to package instructions, then drain.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter, stir in flour, and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until thickened.
  4. Add cheese, stir until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Mix cooked pasta with cheese sauce and serve warm.
