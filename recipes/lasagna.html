Delicious Lasagna

Description

This easy and delicious lasagna is packed with layers of meat sauce, ricotta, and melted cheese. Perfect for family dinners or meal prep.

It only takes 35 to 45 minutes from start to finish, making it a great choice for a quick and hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 jar pasta sauce (24 oz)
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 ½ cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground meat in a pan until browned, then add pasta sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Boil noodles according to package directions, or use oven-ready noodles.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta with egg and Parmesan cheese (if using).
  5. In a baking dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 10-15 minutes.
  7. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
