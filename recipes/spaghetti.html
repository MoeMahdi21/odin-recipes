Easy Spaghetti

Description

This easy spaghetti recipe is a quick go-to for busy weeknights. It combines cooked pasta with a rich tomato sauce and simple seasonings for a satisfying meal.

Ready in just 30 minutes, it’s a family favorite that doesn’t require much prep.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti noodles
  • 1 jar pasta sauce (24 oz)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti noodles according to package instructions, then drain.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add pasta sauce and simmer for 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Mix spaghetti with sauce and serve hot. Top with Parmesan if desired.
